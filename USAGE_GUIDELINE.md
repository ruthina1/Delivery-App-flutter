# Burger Knight - Usage Guideline

## Getting Started

### Installation
1. Download the APK file (`app-release.apk`) to your Android device
2. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device settings
3. Tap on the APK file to install
4. Open the app from your app drawer

### First Launch
1. **Splash Screen**: The app will display the Burger Knight logo briefly
2. **Onboarding**: Swipe through 3 screens to learn about the app features:
   - Discover Delicious: Explore our burger menu
   - Easy Ordering: Simple ordering process
   - Fast Delivery: Quick delivery service
3. Tap "Get Started" to proceed

## Account Setup

### Sign Up
1. Tap "Sign Up" on the login screen
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm Password
3. Tap "Create Account"
4. You'll be automatically logged in

### Sign In
1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap "Sign In"
3. Forgot password? Tap "Forgot Password?" (if available)

## Main Features

### Home Screen
- **Location**: Tap the location icon to change delivery address
- **Search**: Tap the search bar to find specific items
- **Categories**: Scroll horizontally to browse burger categories
- **Popular Items**: View trending burgers in a horizontal scroll
- **Featured**: Browse featured items in a grid layout
- **Cart Icon**: Tap to view your cart (shows item count badge)
- **Notifications**: Tap the bell icon for notifications

- **Product Grid**: Browse products in a 2-column grid
- **Search**: Tap the search icon to find items

### Product Details
1. Tap any product card to view details
2. View product information:
   - Name and price
   - Description
   - Ingredients
   - Reviews
3. Adjust quantity using +/- buttons
4. Tap "Add to Cart" to add the item

### Shopping Cart
- **View Cart**: Tap the cart icon in the header or bottom navigation
- **Manage Items**:
  - Increase/decrease quantity with +/- buttons
  - Remove items by tapping the delete icon
  - Clear all items using "Clear All" button
- **Order Summary**:
  - Subtotal: Sum of all items
  - Delivery Fee: Free for orders over 1500 ETB, otherwise 50 ETB
  - Total: Final amount
- **Checkout**: Tap "Checkout" button to proceed

### Checkout
1. **Delivery Address**:
   - Review your current address
   - Tap "Change" to modify address
   - Tap "Add New Address" to add another location
2. **Payment Method**:
   - Select your preferred payment method
   - Options may include Cash on Delivery, Card, etc.
3. **Order Summary**: Review your order details
4. **Place Order**: Tap "Place Order" to confirm

### Orders
- **View Orders**: Tap the orders icon in bottom navigation
- **Order Status**:
  - Active: Orders being prepared or in transit
  - Completed: Past orders
- **Order Details**: Tap any order to view:
  - Order number
  - Items ordered
  - Delivery address
  - Payment method
  - Status timeline
- **Actions**:
  - Track Order: View real-time delivery status
  - Reorder: Quickly order the same items again

### Profile
- **My Profile**: View and edit your account information
- **Settings**: Adjust app preferences
- **Payment Methods**: Manage saved payment options
- **Addresses**: Manage delivery addresses
- **Favorites**: View saved favorite items
- **Help Center**: Access support and FAQs
- **Privacy Policy & Terms**: View legal information
- **Logout**: Sign out of your account

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open
- Restart your device
- Reinstall the app
- Check device storage space

### Can't Add Items to Cart
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check internet connection
- Restart the app

### Payment Issues
- Verify payment method details
- Check internet connection
- Try a different payment method

### Delivery Address Issues
- Ensure address is complete
- Check location permissions
- Update address in Profile section
